## Overview

Team Settings is your central hub for configuring how your team operates within the Business Process Framework. Here you can manage your team profile, set preferences, and customize features to match your organization's needs.

## Accessing Team Settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Team Menu">
    Click on your team name in the top navigation bar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Team Settings">
    From the dropdown menu, click **Team Settings**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate Settings Sections">
    Use the left sidebar to access different settings categories:
    - General
    - Members
    - AI Configuration
    - Features
    - Preferences
    - Billing (Owners only)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Team Profile Settings

### Team Name

Your team name is displayed throughout the application and helps members identify which team they are working in.

- **Editing**: Click the edit icon next to your team name
- **Requirements**: 3-50 characters, alphanumeric and spaces allowed
- **Visibility**: Visible to all team members

<Tip>
Choose a team name that clearly identifies your organization or department to avoid confusion if members belong to multiple teams.
</Tip>

### Team Description

Provide context about your team's purpose and scope.

- **Use cases**: Describe your team's mission, the types of processes you manage, or who should join
- **Visibility**: Visible to all team members
- **Length**: Up to 500 characters

### Team Logo

Upload a custom logo to personalize your team's identity.

- **Supported formats**: PNG, JPG, SVG
- **Recommended size**: 200x200 pixels
- **Maximum file size**: 2MB
- **Usage**: Displayed in navigation and on team pages

### Team Settings Information

View important information about your team:

- **Created date**: When the team was established
- **Team ID**: Unique identifier for support purposes
- **Current plan**: Your subscription tier
- **Member count**: Total active team members

## Team Preferences

### Default Time Zone

Set the time zone used for displaying dates and times throughout the application.

- **Affects**: Scheduled workflows, report timestamps, activity logs
- **Per-user override**: Individual members can set their own time zone preference
- **Common usage**: Set to your headquarters location or most common member time zone

### Date Format

Choose how dates are displayed:

- **MM/DD/YYYY** (US format)
- **DD/MM/YYYY** (International format)
- **YYYY-MM-DD** (ISO format)

### Workflow Settings

Configure how workflows operate across your team:

- **Default approval timeout**: How long before approval requests escalate (1-30 days)
- **Auto-assignment**: Enable automatic task assignment based on roles
- **Sequential vs. parallel**: Default workflow execution mode
- **Notification frequency**: How often to remind about pending tasks

### KPI Reporting

Set preferences for KPI tracking and reporting:

- **Reporting frequency**: Weekly, monthly, or quarterly
- **Report delivery**: Who receives automated KPI reports
- **Default metrics**: Which KPIs to track by default for new processes
- **Comparison periods**: How to display trends (vs. previous period, vs. baseline, etc.)

## Privacy and Security

### Data Retention

Configure how long to retain different types of data:

- **Activity logs**: 30, 60, 90 days, or indefinitely
- **Archived processes**: 6 months, 1 year, or indefinitely
- **Assessment results**: 1 year, 3 years, or indefinitely

<Warning>
Reducing retention periods will permanently delete older data. Ensure you have exported any necessary information before shortening retention periods.
</Warning>

### Team Visibility

Control what information is visible across your team:

- **Member directory**: Show all team members to everyone
- **Process visibility**: Default visibility for new processes (team-wide or private)
- **KPI transparency**: Who can view team-wide KPIs
- **Assessment results**: Who can see individual assessment results

### Two-Factor Authentication

<Accordion title="Require 2FA for All Members">
When enabled, all team members must set up two-factor authentication to access the team.

**Benefits**:
- Enhanced security for sensitive business data
- Compliance with security policies
- Protection against account compromise

**Considerations**:
- Members must have access to a 2FA device (phone, authenticator app)
- May require support for setting up 2FA
- Grace period of 7 days for existing members to enable
</Accordion>

## Customization Options

### Custom Fields

Create custom fields to capture team-specific information:

- **Process metadata**: Add fields relevant to your industry or workflow
- **Member attributes**: Track additional information about team members
- **Assessment categories**: Customize assessment groupings

### Templates

Configure default templates for your team:

- **Process templates**: Pre-configured process structures
- **Workflow templates**: Reusable workflow patterns
- **Report templates**: Standardized reporting formats

### Labels and Tags

Set up team-wide labels and tags for organization:

- **Process categories**: Define custom categories for your processes
- **Priority levels**: Customize priority labels
- **Status options**: Add custom status values
- **Department tags**: Tag content by department or function

<Tip>
Standardizing labels and tags across your team makes it easier to find and organize content.
</Tip>

## Integration Settings

### Connected Services

View and manage external integrations:

- **Slack**: Team notifications and updates
- **Microsoft Teams**: Integration with Teams channels
- **Google Workspace**: Calendar and Drive integration

Configure webhooks to send data to external systems:

### API Keys

Generate and manage API keys for custom integrations:

<Warning>

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Access Settings

If you cannot access Team Settings:

- Verify you have Admin or Owner permissions
- Check if you are logged into the correct team
- Try refreshing your browser
- Clear your browser cache and cookies

### Changes Not Saving

If your settings changes are not persisting:

- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you clicked the **Save** button
- Look for error messages or validation warnings
- Try making one change at a time

### Settings Reverted

If settings revert to previous values:

- Verify no other admin changed the settings
- Check the activity log for recent changes
- Contact support if settings change unexpectedly

<Info>
All settings changes are logged in the team activity log, which you can access from the Settings page.
</Info>
