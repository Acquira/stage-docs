This feature is available to **Team Admins** and **Team Owners** only.
</Note>

## Overview

Team Preferences allow you to customize defaults for new members and control team-wide behaviors.

<Info>
This page describes functionality that may be located in your **personal Profile settings** rather than the **Administration > Team Settings** page. The main `Team Settings` page is used for managing members, organization name, and API keys.
</Info>

## Accessing Preferences

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Your Profile Settings">
    Click on your user icon or name in the sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Preferences">
    In your personal profile settings, look for a "Preferences" or "Notifications" tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Sections">
    Your personal preferences may be organized into sections:
    - Notification Defaults
    - Time Zone and Locale
    - Theme (Light/Dark mode)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Make Changes">
    Adjust any preferences as needed and click **Save Changes**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Notification Preferences

While team-wide notification defaults may be set by an Admin, individual users can typically override these in their personal profile settings.

### Notification Types

You can likely control which events trigger notifications:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Workflow & Task Notifications">
    - **Task assigned**: When a workflow task is assigned to you
    - **Task due soon**: Reminder 24 hours before task deadline
    - **Approval required**: When you need to approve a workflow step
    - **Task Comment**: When someone comments on your task
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Team Notifications">
    - **New team member**: When someone joins your team
    - **Role changed**: When your role is changed
    - **Team announcement**: Important team-wide messages
    - **Mention**: When someone mentions you in a comment
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="System Notifications">
    - **KPI reports**: Weekly or monthly KPI summaries
    - **Feature updates**: When new features are available
    - **Maintenance notices**: Scheduled maintenance announcements
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Notification Frequency

Choose how often to send notifications:

- **Real-time**: Send immediately as events occur
- **Daily digest**: Bundle notifications into one email daily
- **Weekly digest**: Send summary every Monday

## Time Zone and Locale

Configure your personal regional settings.

### Default Time Zone

Set the time zone for displaying dates and times:

- **Per-user**: Each member can set their own
- **Common options**:
  - UTC
  - America/New_York (Eastern)
  - America/Chicago (Central)
  - America/Los_Angeles (Pacific)

### Date Format

Choose how dates are displayed:

- **MM/DD/YYYY** (US format)
- **DD/MM/YYYY** (International format)
- **YYYY-MM-DD** (ISO format)

### Time Format

Choose between 12-hour and 24-hour time:

- **12-hour** (3:30 PM)
- **24-hour** (15:30)

## Customization Options

### Theme

Choose the visual theme for your view of the app:

- **Light**: Light background (default)
- **Dark**: Dark background
- **Auto**: Match system settings
