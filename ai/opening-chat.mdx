The AI assistant is always ready to help you with your work. Let's get you started with opening and using the chat interface.

## Finding the Chat Button

Look for the chat button in the bottom-right corner of your screen. It's always there, on every page of the application.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/ai/chat-button.png" alt="AI chat button location" />
</Frame>

<Tip>
The chat button follows you as you scroll, so you can access the AI assistant anytime, from anywhere in the app.
</Tip>

## Opening the Chat Window

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the chat button">
    Click the floating chat button in the bottom-right corner. The chat window will slide open from the right side of your screen.
  </Step>

  <Step title="See your chat interface">
    You'll see your chat history and a message input box at the bottom. The AI is ready to help!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Creating New Chat Sessions

Sometimes you want to start fresh on a new topic. Creating a new session is simple.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'New Chat'">
    Look for the "New Chat" button at the top of the chat window.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start your conversation">
    Your previous conversation is saved, and you now have a clean slate for a new topic.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Each chat session is saved automatically. You can come back to any conversation later.
</Note>

## Switching Between Sessions

You can easily switch between different conversations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="View your sessions">
    Click the menu icon or "Chat History" to see all your previous conversations.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a session">
    Click on any previous conversation to continue where you left off.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Closing the Chat

When you're done, simply click the X button or click anywhere outside the chat window to close it. Your conversation is automatically saved.

<Tip>
Think of chat sessions like different conversations with a helpful colleague. Use separate sessions for different projects or topics to keep things organized.
</Tip>
